Straightening Rules (For Safe & Healthy Hair)

Can I straighten my hair? Answer
How often? 2-3x per week max
Best temp? 300-350°F (never above 380°F)
Can I air dry first? Yes, but apply heat protectant while damp
Can I straighten daily? Not recommended—touch up only
What to do before straightening? Use heat protectant, make sure hair is 100% dry
What to do after? Apply DAE Oil to ends for shine & frizz control
